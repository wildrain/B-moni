General Questions

  What is a Blurb?

A “Blurb” is a short and shareable piece of social media content that provides value to fans. Think of a Facebook post about something your friends would be interested in or an interesting Tweet. Have a gander at our blog post on how we’re re-defining the Blurb for the social era.

  What is a FameRate?

FameRate is both a score given to an indivual Blurb and a cumulative score given to a Blurber or Buyer. It defines their effeciveness in engaging and reaching followers. It consists of differing weights given to social media actions conducted on a Blurb and the reach produced by such actions. The higher the score, the visible the blurb.

  How many buyers can I write for?

As many as we automatically match you up with. Hint: Adjusting your "interests" will match you up with different buyers.

Content

  Do I own the copyright to the content I write?

Yes, but only until it is accepted and posted by the buyer, at which point the copyrite is transferred to them.

  Can’t buyers just steal content?

Well they could, but that wouldn’t be very nice of them. We give you the ability to flag content that you think has been copied or too closely copied. We look into the flagged content and make a decision from there. Three strikes and we boot them off the platform.

  How many Blurbs can I write per day?

As many as you want. However, we do give the buyers the ability to flag content that they feel is spammy. If you spam three times we boot you off the site! Sorry! ...but not really.

  What happens to all of the hard work I put into content that wasn’t accepted?

We give you the option of writing each Blurb at up to 5 different clients, thus giving your blurbs a higher chance of being selected. Once one client accepts the blurb it disappears from the other buyers' feeds. You can always re-purpose the same content in the future by adding a different title/hashtags, then submitting it to a different buyer.

Payment

  How much money can I make?

This depends on how often you post. You'll make from 75c-80c per blurb at the moment. A blurb should take you anywhere from 2-5 minutes. You can spend an hour a day and make around $15-20.

  How does payment work?

1. In the top right you can see how much you've earned since your last deposit.

2. You select a deposit threshold, or in other words how much money you need to make before payment is deposited in your account. A $10 deposit threshold requires a 0.37c transaction fee. The lowest deposit threshold without a transaction fee is $20.

3. At the end of every month we'll send you a notification (the little bell) informing you that you can now deposit your money.

4. Go to your deposits page and follow the instructions to deposit your earnings via Paypal

5. You can view your deposit progress by hovering over the credit card icon in the top right.
