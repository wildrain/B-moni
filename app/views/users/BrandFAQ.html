General Questions

  What is a Blurb?

A “Blurb” is a short and shareable piece of social media content that provides value to fans. Think of a Facebook post about something your friends would be interested in or an interesting Tweet. Have a gander at our blog post on how we’re re-defining the Blurb for the social era.

  What is a Blurber?

Someone who writes Blurbs. In other words, the people who write the content that will engage your social media followers.

  What is FameRate ?

FameRate is both a score given to an indivual Blurb and a cumulative score given to a Blurber or Buyer. It defines their effeciveness in engaging and reaching followers. It consists of differing weights given to social media actions conducted on a Blurb and the reach produced by such actions. The higher the score, the visible the blurb.

  What if I don’t want people to know my brand is using Blurbi?

We will soon be rolling out an anonymous feature which will give you the option of remaining anonymous. You still have to define your voice and brand message. Writers will leave out mentioning your brand’s name by just placing ____ in its place, from which you can then add your brand’s name.

  How many Blurbers can I employ?

We match you up with only the most relevant blurbers. On average you'll have between 5-10 blurbers writing for you.

Content

  How do I know the Blurbers are writing quality content?

They’re all hand-picked by us through a specific process that weeds out poor writers and isolates the best social media content writers. Tip: make sure to look at a Blurber's FameRate to see how successful their blurbs are.

  Can’t I just steal content?

Well you could, but that wouldn’t be very nice now would it. We give our Blurbers the ability to flag content that they think has been copied or too closely copied. We look into the flagged content and make a decision from there. Three strikes and we boot you off the platform. Sorry! ...but not really

  Do I own the copyright to the content?

Yes, but only after you pay for it. Until then, the writer of the content reserves all copyright.

  How many Blurbers can I employ?

As many as you’d like. However, they have to accept to write for you as well.

  How many Blurbs can I post per day?

As many as you like.

Payment

  How much will it cost?

A buck a blurb! If you post 2 blurbs a day you'll spend roughly $60 /month. 3 blurbs roughly $90/month.

  How does payment work?

1.   Upon signing up you receive a few free blurbs. The rest you'll have to pay for.

2.    You make deposits into your account (minimum $10).

3.    Every time you post a Blurb the defined amount per-blurb is subtracted from your account.

4.    You can view your account balance by hovering over the Credit Card icon in the top right corner.

5.     Hint: Check the auto-renew function so that your account always stays topped up.
