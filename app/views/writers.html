It's all in the Blurb

Blurbing involves finding relevant content for a buyer's social media pages, then writing copy around the content, presenting it engagingly to the buyer's fans. A Blurb is part curation and part writing.

How exactly does Blurbi work?

Brands and writers are matched up according to communities.

Writers curate/write engaging content and interact with followers.

Brands accept/edit content to be posted to their social media channels.

Brands pay the writers a small fee.
