How exactly does Blurbi work?

Businesses and writers are matched up according to their interests and expertise.

Businesses define their voice and how much they want to pay for each piece of content.

Writers curate/write engaging content and interact with followers.

Businesses review content and select which they'd like to post to their social media channels. They only pay for the content they use.

Businesses / Agencies

How does it benefit my business?

Save time

The most time consuming task of social media marketing is creating content. Let professional writers do it for you.

Save money

Forget hiring or outsourcing your content. By crowdsourcing content on a pay per use basis you can scale your content at any rate, and at any cost you feel necessary.

More content + better content

Because you have as many writers writing for you as you want, you’ll be privy to a wider range of content. Since these writers are all vetted professionals, you can be sure that your content will be extra engaging.

Manage several accounts

With an Agency account you have the ability to set up and manage content for as many Businesses as you represent.
